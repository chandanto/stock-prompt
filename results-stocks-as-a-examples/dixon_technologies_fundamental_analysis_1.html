About the Company
Dixon Technologies is India's largest Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) company. Incorporated in 1993, listed on NSE/BSE in 2017. It manufactures mobile phones, LED TVs, washing machines, lighting products, wearables, CCTV, and IT hardware for global brands including Xiaomi, Motorola, Oppo, Samsung, Philips, and HP. Mobile & EMS is now 84% of revenue (up from 43% in FY23).
HQ: Noida, UP · Auditor: Singhi & Co + SN Dhawan & Co LLP · ISIN: INE935N01020
